# Compliance Checklist

## Sensitive Personal Information

- Treat cycle records, symptoms, pain level, and mood as sensitive health information.
- Separate privacy policy consent from sensitive data consent.
- Log consent version, purpose, granted time, and withdrawal time.
- Provide export and deletion pathways inside the product.

## Product Copy Guardrails

- Avoid diagnostic wording.
- Use phrases such as "estimated window", "possible delay", and "consider tracking changes".
- Do not imply treatment efficacy or clinical interpretation.

## Release Readiness

- Prepare mini program filing materials for mainland China launch.
- Publish privacy policy, user agreement, and data retention statement.
- Set internal retention periods for analytics events and deleted-user tombstones.
- Define breach notification and customer support workflow.

## Engineering Guardrails

- Minimize fields collected in v1.
- Do not collect sexual activity, fertility plans, or clinical history by default.
- Keep analytics events free of free-text symptom notes.
- Store privacy actions in an auditable log.
